My Education

St Josephs School

St. Josephs School was established in 1955 by its founder Principal Late Miss Bertha Pereira as a Primary School, catering to the need for a personal touch in primary education where it is imperative to know the child before you attempt to educate him. With the passage of time, it soon became evident that primary education fulfilled this need only upto a point. In order to maintain the rapport established between teacher & pupil in the primary school, it became necessary to venture into secondary education. In June 1982 the secondary section first saw the light of day with a fledging staff and 45 students. Over the years the school has grown from strength to strength and has acquired an enviable reputation in the field of education. It is administered by St. Dominic Education Trust.

Atharva College of Engineering

The Atharva Educational Trust’ owns spacious campus in Malad, where an ideal educational ‘Atharva College of Engineering’ campus has grown in leaps and bound in short span of time. The Institute has started functioning from the academic year 1999-2000 & has achieved good results in the University of Mumbai since then. The institution has developed laboratories with sophisticated equipments. Learned, disciplined and qualified staff is going to be another attraction for the students in this institution.Atharva was established in the year 1999 for the noble cause of Engineering education in western suburbs.
